Race Week at Les Noës Blanches

A place to relax, a place to entertain.

If you are planning a le Mans race week it is likely you are either coming as part of a competing team, or as part of an organisation that needs to show some people a good time.

At les Noes Blanches, we have the house, the proximity to the circuit and the grounds to be able to run the race week exactly the way you want it.

The Essentials - what is included

  • A Chateau & Some Beautiful Grounds

    Your base for the race week, the chateau has 9 bedrooms and can accomodate 15 people. With 12 acres of grounds set in 450 acres of woodland - we also have an optional 170m2 marquee setup to accomodate many more guests for entertaining and events.


  • An In-house Organiser

    Need to organise transportation back from the circuit, get a dinner sorted after coming back late or a babysitter for a few hours? It helps to have someone local on the ground who speaks english and who knows their way around. Included in the race week package is access to someone who can help plan your stay and handle any last-minute requests the house guests may have as and when they come.

The Dates

The standard package runs from Tuesday before the race to the Monday after and accomodates up to 15 people in 9 bedrooms. Naturally if you require the dates shifted or extended we will do our utmost to accomodate you. The house is provided with a cleaner who will remake the rooms and tidy the premises each day, but is otherwise self-catering. Extra services (such as in-house cook, babysitter etc) can be organised at the time of booking or through your organiser nearer the time.

The Optional Extras

  • An In-house Chef

    Whether it's early starts and late finishes, or down-time relaxing with friends and colleagues - it's likely you'll want someone to look after the shopping and the cooking some of the time. Our in-house chef can work to your schedule and help organise everything from a barbeque to a sumptuous multi-course dinner.

  • An Event Organiser

    You've got this great space, maybe you should show it off? Whether organising an informal barbecue and a big screen to watch a world-cup match, or a fully-catered dinner for over 100 guests, you can book our english-speaking event organiser who can source what you'll need and when you'll need it.

  • An Event Marquee

    You have the option of making use of our 170m2 pagoda-style marquee to accomodate events, dinners and parties. Please ask for more info.

  • Helicopter Landing

    If you can, then you should. The in-house organiser will put in the necessary calls to get landing clearance to arrive on the Chateau grounds in style.
