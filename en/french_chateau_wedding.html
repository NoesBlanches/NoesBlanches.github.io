French Château Wedding at Château les Noës Blanches

An Elegant Outdoor Summer Wedding

Weddings are an important part of what we do at Château les Noës Blanches, and with the outdoor chateau wedding season running from May through to September, demand can be high. We only take one booking per week in order to ensure enough time to settle in and prepare for the big day, and while a wedding in the sunshine amongst the gardens of a chateau provides a romantic backdrop, we can also help organise events and activities for you and your guests, as well as accommodation for those not staying at the house.

The house, grounds and pagoda marquee are all equipped with the necessities (furniture, coolers, equipped catering tent etc) to comfortably handle seated dining events for up to around 170 guests.

We are able to cover the full range of services - from catering though to wedding planning - but nothing is imposed, so you are free to pick and choose the parts that suit you and your budget. The château itself is designed to accommodate 15 guests, but we have a comprehensive list of local guest houses, gîtes and hotels that you can find here to house the remainder of your guests very near-by.





Weddings at Château les Noës Blanches

Wedding château weekend - An idea of how it happens

From the first arrivals, the practice dinner, the welcome barbecue, the Day itself, and till the last of the guests head off ~ here are a few pictures to animate how a wedding event at Château les Noës Blanches comes together.

Pagoda Marquee

A summer wedding classic, our pagoda marquee can accommodate up to around 170 for dinner and more for dancing. Earlier in the day the sides can be opened up to let your guests drink in the view, and later they can be dropped to make for a more intimate ambiance.

House, gardens and grounds

The house and grounds available on an exclusive basis for the duration of your summer wedding weekend. The house can accommodate up to 15 guests ~ close friends and family. Find out more about château accommodation

In-house catering

We have our very experienced in-house caterer that we offer to all our guests. We offer a range of menus and service options and we also don't charge corkage (should you wish to provide your own wines). All our options have the price of service staff included. If you prefer to go with your own caterer then that's fine too.

Fully-equipped catering kitchen (catering tent)

We provide a dedicated catering space adjacent to the main marquee. Should you chose to use your own caterers, we can also fully equip it.

Tables & Chairs

Classic white wooden chairs with satin pads ~ it's a wedding after all! The round tables are standard wedding size and can accommodate around 8-10 places each.

Linen & Tableware

Extra accommodation

We have good relations with all the local hotels and guesthouses, and can provide a comprehensive list of near-by options available to your guests.

Pre & post-wedding events

A welcome barbecue? A post-wedding picnic? A wine tasting or a tour of the racing circuit? You name it we can arrange it for you.

Car parking

A detail, but you don't want your uncle's car in the background of all your photos, right? We have a dedicated area for car parking that keeps them nice and safe - and out of the way.

Let's discuss your plans!

Are you organising an event?

To make every event a success, we like to get as much of the preparation done in advance - leaving you to concentrate on the finishing touches that make your event completely unique.
