Time with your friends & family and a setting for beautiful understated events. Welcome to your Château in the Western Loire.

Welcome to Chateau les Noës Blanches. Situated next to the iconic motorsports town of le Mans and extending into the open countryside of the Western Loire Valley, the estate stretches across 450 acres of gardens and private grounds. The house is fully equipped for entertaining and provides an elegant setting for events and perfect place to spend time amongst your family and friends.

20 minutes from le Mans, 4 hours from London St. Pancras by Eurostar and 45 minutes from Paris by TGV fast train - shopping trips to Paris are a breeze.

Chateau les Noes Blanches: Quick tour

The house first appears on archive documents around 1540, during the reign of king Francis I, but the original building is likely much older. Like any venerable old house, with each generation it has undergone modification and refurbishment to bring it up to date for the time, and this generation has been no exception.

The courtyard

The heart of the estate, on a warm summer's day you always want to stop by the main fountain for a while.

Provençal garden and pool

Take a dip in the sparkly heated swimming Pool in the provençal garden when the summer days get warm.

Al fresco

A dinner out in the open amongst friends. What could be better?

Wood-panelled dining room

Warm and intimate, this is where many a dinner has wound up going on into the early hours.

The kitchen

The natural hub of the house in the morning, the kitchen is wholly equipped to cater for a full house of 15 guests. On colder days, we'll get a fire going in the open fireplace - there's a good reserve of logs kept for this reason.

Main living room

High ceilings and views across the estate, on summer afternoons we'll open open the windows and let the breeze run in from across the fields.

The tennis court

A quick game before sundown? Maybe work up a bit of an appetite...

Second Living Room / TV lounge

A second living room to get away from the others, or an opportunity to put on a film and kick back.

White room

Light and breezy, with a huge marble fireplace and an extravagant bathroom overlooking the boating lake.

Maroc room

Tapered ceilings and light from all sides, this room has views across the gardens and out to the forest.

Corsair room

Up in the tower, you can almost smell the ocean breeze from up here.

Racing room for a le Mans Chateau

Motorsport is part of the local heritage, and this room is bedecked with vintage posters of the classic races of the past.

Garden room

One of our smallest rooms, but very out-of-the-way, the garden room also has one of the best views in the house.

Colonial room

A large room with a beautifully-intricate Balinese carved mahogany bed and views over the courtyard.

Boudoir room

Bijou and comfortable, this room looks out over the fountain and the courtyard and has an adjoining door through to the green room

Green room

A heavy french mahogany bed and views over the courtyard, this room connects directly to the boudoir room.
