WELCOME TO LES NOËS BLANCHES

Chic & Relaxed in the Pays de la Loire

Welcome to les Noës Blanches, our classic country chateau near le Mans in the Pays de la Loire region of France. Nestled in 450 acres of gardens and private estate, the house is fully equipped for entertaining and provides an elegant and distinguished backdrop for events, weddings or simply a few days amongst friends and family.

The house is a 20 minute drive from the centre of le Mans which is, in turn, about 4 hours by Eurostar from London St. Pancras. Le Mans is also on the TGV train network and under an hour from Paris - making day trips a breeze.

Quick tour of the house

The house first appears on archive documents around 1540, during the reign of king Francis I, but the original building is in all likelihood much older. Like any venerable old house, with each generation it has undergone modification and refurbishment to bring it up to date for the time, and this generation has been no exception.

    Racing room

The grounds and gardens

The grounds and gardens extend out 450 acres in all directions around the house. From the fountain in the courtyard, to the lawns and gardens, and on further to the oak and pine forests beyond.
