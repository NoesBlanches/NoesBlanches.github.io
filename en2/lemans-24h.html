Le Mans 24hrs Race Week at Château les Noës Blanches

If you are planning a le Mans race week it is likely you are either coming as part of a competing team, or as part of an organisation that needs to show some people a good time. At le Mans Château les Noes Blanches, we have the beautiful château, the proximity to the circuit and the grounds to be able to run the race week exactly the way you want it.

A le Mans Château & Some Beautiful Grounds

Your base for the race week, the chateau has 9 bedrooms and can accommodate 15 people. With gardens set in 450 acres of grounds and woodland - we also have an optional 170m2 marquee setup to accommodate many more guests for entertaining and events.

An In-house Organiser

Need to organise transportation back from the circuit, get a dinner sorted after coming back late or a babysitter for a few hours? It helps to have someone local on the ground who speaks english and who knows their way around. Included in the race week package is access to someone who can help plan your stay and handle any last-minute requests the house guests may have as and when they come.

An Event Marquee

You have the option of making use of our 170m2 pagoda-style marquee to accomodate events, dinners and parties. Please ask for more info.

An In-house Chef

Whether it's early starts and late finishes, or down-time relaxing with friends and colleagues - it's likely you'll want someone to look after the shopping and the cooking some of the time. Our in-house chef can work to your schedule and help organise everything from a barbeque to a sumptuous multi-course dinner.

An Event Organiser

You've got this great space, maybe you should show it off? Whether organising an informal barbecue and a big screen to watch a world-cup match, or a fully-catered dinner for over 100 guests, you can book our english-speaking event organiser who can source what you'll need and when you'll need it.

Helicopter Landing

If you can, then you should. The in-house organiser will put in the necessary calls to get landing clearance to arrive on the Chateau grounds quickly and safely.

Are you organising an le Mans race event?

To make every event a success, we like to get as much of the preparation done in advance - leaving you to concentrate on the finishing touches that make your event completely unique.
